ABOUT US

City Max Movie Theater in Sri Lanka is transforming the cinema experience by combining traditional charm with modern technology and comfort.
The theater offers an exciting and effortless entertainment experience, matching international standards. With premium screens, quality audio equipment and comfortable chair.The movie experience is unforgettable for all ages. City Max is a presentation resort offering more exciting offerings.

offers clients from all walks of life innovative solutions, challenging
industry norms.

Mission

to provide a range of varied, satisfying experiences to consumers via
a portfolio of brands propelled by an encouraging corporate culture that
seeks to optimize value for all parties involved.
